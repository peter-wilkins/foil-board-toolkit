# Legal And Ethics Boundary

## Rule

Learn from the market. Do not clone the market.

## Allowed

- Record published dimensions with source links.
- Analyse broad design trends.
- Compare ratios such as length-to-volume and width-to-rider-weight.
- Use photographs for qualitative observations.
- Generate original parametric geometry.
- Attribute every external source.

## Not Allowed

- Copy a commercial board's exact outline, rocker, rails, or bottom contours.
- Republish proprietary CAD, scans, or paid plan files.
- Present generated designs as replicas of named boards.
- Use non-commercial licensed assets for commercial workshop/client work.

## Wording To Use

Good:

> Inspired by public trends in modern downwind foil boards.

Avoid:

> Generates a Kalama Barracuda clone.

## Open Question

Generated design files may need their own licence separate from the software licence. Apache-2.0 is fine for source code, but workshop output policy should be decided before a public launch.
